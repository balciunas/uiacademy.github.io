Description

In KAYAK UI Academy is a collection of innovative agile focused lectures. Any lecture is carried out once a group of 8 people join the queue. The academy consists of three categories:

  • Basics - all lectures require a minimal level of knowledge, these lectures will provide it.
  • Practical - focused workshops to offer a solution to a raised problem.
  • Theoretical - we will dive into theory behind the UI why and how it is done.
The goal is to market and grow the academy based on the needs of the students. Optimizing both Lecturer and Student time to reach the maximum results.
